# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people[cite: 3].
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences[cite: 3].
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback[cite: 3].

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks[cite: 3].
* Public or private harassment[cite: 3].
* Publishing others' private information without explicit permission[cite: 3].

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository owner. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly[cite: 3].
